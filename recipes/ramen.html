Delicious Ramen

Description

This fan favorite is as easy to make as they come. A salty treat with a classic chicken taste that is sure to elicit some nostalgic memories.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of water
  • Packet of noodles
  • Seasoning Packet

Steps

  1. Boil 2 cups of water in a sauce pan. Add noodles, breaking up if desired. Cook for 3 minutesor until noodles are tender, stirring occasionally.
  2. Remove from heat. Stir in seasonings from flavor packet. To lower sodium, use less seasoning.
